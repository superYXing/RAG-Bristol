## Update your home address

To update your home address:

1. Log in to [Student Info](https://www.bris.ac.uk/studentinfo).
2. On the left-hand menu, select "Home Address".
3. Select the "Update" button and fill in your new details.
4. Press "Save".

For international students, this information must be the address in your home country with which you are registered for the university.

This must not be the address of an agent.

If you have any questions, contact [student-services@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-services@bristol.ac.uk) .

## Update your current term-time address

To update your term-time address:

1. Log in to [Student Info](https://www.bris.ac.uk/studentinfo).
2. On the left-hand menu, select "Current Address".
3. Select the "Update" button and fill in your new details.
4. Press "Save".

If you live in University-managed accommodation, do not add your room or flat number.

## Update your emergency contact details

To update your emergency contact details (next of kin):

1. Log in to [Student Info](https://www.bris.ac.uk/studentinfo).
2. On the left-hand menu, select "Emergency Contact".
3. Select the "Update" button and fill in the new details.
4. Press "Save".

## Update your personal email address

To update your personal (non-university) email address:

1. Log in to [Student Info](https://www.bris.ac.uk/studentinfo).
2. On the left-hand menu, select "Home Address".
3. Select the "Update" button and fill in the new details.
4. Press "Save".

## Student Visa holders

If you are a Student Visa holder. you must update the University on any changes to your:

* UK contact address
* telephone number
* emergency contact details.

This is part of your rights and responsibilities and is a UKVI requirement.
