> Where to find information you need online and contacts for our Student Services team and your school.

## Student support

[Search online support](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/). Popular topics are [finances](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/), [wellbeing](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/wellbeing/), [studies](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/), [life in Bristol](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/).

If you cannot see your tuition fee on your financial record, [contact your faculty](#faculty). They will add your fee to your record.

If you can't find the information you are looking for, contact the Student Services team.

In person services: Monday to Friday, 9 am to 4 pm.   
Telephony and email services: Monday to Friday, 9 am to 4:30 pm.  
Email: [student-services@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-services@bristol.ac.uk)  
Phone: [+44 (0)117 428 3000](tel:+441174283000)

To speak to a team member in person, visit the front desk on the upper ground floor of [Senate House](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Senate+House+Study+Centre/@51.4587949,-2.6040569,19.62z/data=!4m5!3m4!1s0x0:0x9627166d401816ca!8m2!3d51.4589784!4d-2.6034359).

## Contact your school or faculty

Contact your school office if you need help with course or academic queries. This includes exam and assessment arrangements, specialist teaching spaces (labs and workshops), and academic concerns such as suspension or transferring course.

If you are unsure which faculty you are in, [check on Student Info](https://www.bris.ac.uk/studentinfo/).

There are [different contacts if you are studying at Bristol as part of the incoming study abroad programme](/students/support/help/incoming-study-abroad/).
