> There are some course-specific funding opportunities for medicine, dentistry, veterinary science and dental hygiene.

* [Medicine and dentistry](/students/support/finances/med-dent-vet/medicine-dentistry/)
* [Veterinary science](/students/support/finances/med-dent-vet/veterinary-science/)
* [Intercalation](/students/support/finances/med-dent-vet/intercalation/)

If you are a student on one of these courses, you may be able to apply to the NHS [Learning Support Fund](https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/nhs-learning-support-fund). It covers:

* [Travel and accommodation costs](https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/learning-support-fund/travel-and-dual-accommodation-expenses) for practice placements
* Financial support for [students who have children](https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/learning-support-fund/child-dependants-allowance)
* [Financial hardship](https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/learning-support-fund/exceptional-support-fund)
