These fees are based on recommendations made by Research Councils UK (now part of [UK Research and Innovation](https://www.ukri.org/)).

Fees are likely to change each year to reflect updated recommendations.

## Home student fees for 2025/26

Home postgraduate research fees payable for 2025/26

| --- | --- | --- |
| 2025/26 | £4,850 | £2,425 |

## Home and EU student fees for previous years

EU nationals starting their studies from academic year 2021/22 may be classified as Home or Overseas for tuition fee purposes. This will depend on individual circumstances and will be determined following an assessment of your individual [fee status](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/fee-status/).

## Bench fees

Some programmes charge a bench fee in addition to tuition fees.

Bench fees are charged when a programme or particular project incurs specific costs, such as specialist laboratory equipment or fieldwork.

If you need to pay bench fees, they will be listed on the [prospectus entry](/study/postgraduate/find-programme/) and on your offer letter.
