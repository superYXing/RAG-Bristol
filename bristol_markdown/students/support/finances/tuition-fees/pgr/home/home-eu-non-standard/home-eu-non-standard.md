## EU student fees for 2021/22

EU students starting their studies in the academic year 2021/22 are no longer eligible for home fee status.

EU students will be charged [overseas fees from 2021/22 onwards](/students/support/finances/tuition-fees/pgr/overseas/overseas-non-standard/ "Overseas%20non-standard%20programme%20fees").

EU students who started in 2020/21 will pay the 2020/21 starters fee listed in the table above.

## EU student fees for 2021/22
