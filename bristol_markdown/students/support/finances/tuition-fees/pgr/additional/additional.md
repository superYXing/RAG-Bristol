If your PhD extends beyond the normal programme duration or if you resubmit a thesis, you may need to pay additional fees.

Additional postgraduate research fees for 2025/26

| Fee type | Description | 2025/26 fee |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Continuing research fee | Payable to end of funded period if this is longer than the minimum period of study specified for the programme; applies to full-time and part-time students. | In line with agreed fee for programme (pro-rata of the full-time fee) |
| Writing-up fee | Payable by students writing up beyond the maximum period of study specified for the programme. | £580 |
| Re-submission fee | Payable on re-submitting a research degree thesis for re-examination. | £440 |
