## 2021/22 postgraduate research tuition fees

From 2021/22 Channel Islands and Isle of Man students (new and continuing) [pay the same fees as Home students](/students/support/finances/tuition-fees/pgr/home/).

## Previous year fees

Channel Islands and Isle of Man postgraduate research fees payable for the previous two academic years

| Academic year | Full-time fee |
| --- | --- |
| 2020/21 | £9,365 (inc £5,000 supplement) |
| 2019/20 | £9,300 (inc £5,000 supplement) |

These fees are based on recommendations made by Research Councils UK (now part of [UK Research and Innovation](https://www.ukri.org/)).

Fees are likely to change each year to reflect updated recommendations.

## Bench fees

Some programmes charge a bench fee in addition to tuition fees. Bench fees are charged when a programme or particular project incurs specific costs, such as specialist laboratory equipment or field work. If you need to pay bench fees, they will be listed on the [prospectus entry](/study/postgraduate/find-programme/) and on your offer letter.
