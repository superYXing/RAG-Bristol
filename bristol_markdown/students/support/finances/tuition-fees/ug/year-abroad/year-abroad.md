These fees apply if you are spending a year studying or working abroad as part of your University of Bristol degree programme.

Tuition fees for full-time undergraduates on study abroad, work abroad or industrial placement programme in 2025/26

| Type of programme | % of fee charged for home students | % of fee charged for overseas students |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Study abroad for a whole year or a combination of study and work abroad for a whole year (including School of Modern Languages students) | 15% | 50% |
| Industrial placement for whole year | 20% | 25% |
| Other study abroad combinations (including study abroad for a single semester) | 100% | 100% |
