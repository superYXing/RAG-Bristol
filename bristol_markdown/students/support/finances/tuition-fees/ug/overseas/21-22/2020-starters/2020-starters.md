Tuition fees are reviewed annually and likely to increase. Please budget for up to a 5% increase per year.

Part-time fees are charged on a pro rata basis.

## Medicine, Dentistry and Veterinary Science tuition fees

Your tuition fees for Dentistry (BDS), Medicine (MBChB) and Veterinary Sciences (BVSc) will be the same for each year of your programme covering both pre-clinical and clinical years of study.
