### EU students who started in 2021/22

EU nationals starting their studies from academic year 2021/22 may be classified as Home or Overseas for tuition fee purposes. This will depend on individual circumstances and will be determined following an assesment of your individual [fee status](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/fee-status/).
