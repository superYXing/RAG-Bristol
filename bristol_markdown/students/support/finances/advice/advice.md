## [Students living with dependents](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/advice/dependants/)

Any student with children or dependant adults may be eligible for extra financial assistance.

## [Student funding and Sharia law](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/advice/sharia-law/)

Some Muslim students and members of other faiths may be deterred from taking out the UK government's student loans, due to the interest payment system.

### Opening times

If you have a funding worry or query, join a private online drop-in with a Student Money Adviser.

Monday to Friday:

* [10 am to 11 am](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_ZGM0ZGVmYTgtZGUzZi00ZDZjLWIxMTctODYyNTIzNDk5YTdl%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22b2e47f30-cd7d-4a4e-a5da-b18cf1a4151b%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%228935e5e7-c59d-401f-ab88-dcd2d56714bb%22%7d)
* [2 pm to 3 pm.](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_ZGM0ZGVmYTgtZGUzZi00ZDZjLWIxMTctODYyNTIzNDk5YTdl%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22b2e47f30-cd7d-4a4e-a5da-b18cf1a4151b%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%228935e5e7-c59d-401f-ab88-dcd2d56714bb%22%7d)

No need to book, select the link of the drop-in you want to attend, and an adviser will be with you shortly.

Or you can contact us by email: [money-advice@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:money-advice@bristol.ac.uk).

You can also book a 1 to 1, 15-minute appointment with a Money Advisor by calling Student Services on [+44 (0)117 428 3000](tel:+441174283000). Appointments are held via MS Teams or by phone.

## Funding support

* [Digital support fund

  Students can apply to the Digital Support Fund for financial help towards the costs of IT equipment needed for their course. This fund is only available to students who cannot purchase this equipment because of financial difficulties.](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/emergency-funds/digital-support-fund-/)
* [Financial assistance fund

  A hardship fund that provides a non-repayable grant from the University of Bristol to help students who may be in financial difficulties.](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/emergency-funds/financial-assistance-fund/)
* [Disability funding

  Funding is available to help support you with your studies, whether you are a UK home or EU/international student.](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/wellbeing/disability-support/funding/)
* [Bristol SU’s activity hardship fund

  A hardship fund to help with Bristol SU society and activity costs, available to all students. Get support with membership fees, events and kit.](https://www.bristolsu.org.uk/support-centre/support-for-committees/group-finance/activity-hardship-fund)
