> Any student with children under 15, or under 17 if they have special educational needs, or dependant adults may be eligible for extra financial assistance.

## Students from England

You can apply for extra support from Student Finance England.

* [Childcare Grant](https://www.gov.uk/childcare-grant) - Full-time undergraduate students eligible for student finance can apply to receive 85% of their childcare costs or a fixed maximum amount, whichever is less. This grant is paid directly to the student’s childcare provider, which must be registered with Ofsted.
* [Parents’ Learning Allowance](https://www.gov.uk/parents-learning-allowance) - For full-time undergraduate students eligible for student finance who have children.
* [Adult Dependants’ Grant](https://www.gov.uk/adult-dependants-grant) - For full-time undergraduate students eligible for student finance with an adult who depends on them financially.

## Students from Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland

* [Student Finance Wales](https://www.studentfinancewales.co.uk/) supports eligible students from Wales with [Childcare Grants](https://www.studentfinancewales.co.uk/undergraduate-finance/full-time/tuition-fee-and-living-cost-students/what-s-available/childcare-grant/), [Parents’ Learning Allowance](https://www.studentfinancewales.co.uk/undergraduate-finance/part-time/welsh-student/what-s-available/parents-learning-allowance/), and [Adult Dependants’ Grants](https://www.studentfinancewales.co.uk/undergraduate-finance/full-time/tuition-fee-and-living-cost-students/what-s-available/adult-dependants-grant/).
* [Student Finance Northern Ireland](https://www.studentfinanceni.co.uk/) provides funding support to students from Northern Ireland with [Childcare Grants](https://www.studentfinanceni.co.uk/types-of-finance/undergraduate/full-time/tuition-fee-and-living-cost-students/extra-help/childcare-grant/what-is-it/), [Parents’ Learning Allowance](https://www.studentfinanceni.co.uk/types-of-finance/undergraduate/full-time/tuition-fee-and-living-cost-students/extra-help/parents-learning-allowance/what-is-it/), and [Adult Dependants Grants](https://www.studentfinanceni.co.uk/types-of-finance/undergraduate/full-time/tuition-fee-and-living-cost-students/extra-help/adult-dependants-grant/what-is-it/).
* [Student Awards Agency Scotland](https://www.saas.gov.uk/) provides funding support to students from Scotland.

## Universal Credit Benefit

Eligible students (responsible for a child/disabled students) may be able to claim [Universal Credi](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/universal-credit-and-students)[t](https://www.gov.uk/universal-credit) during term time and possibly in full over the summer. Speak to a student [student Money adviser](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/advice/) to find out more.

## Financial Assistance Fund

The [Financial Assistance Fund](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/emergency-funds/financial-assistance-fund/) supports Home (UK) and EU/international undergraduate and postgraduate students with particular financial difficulties. The fund's purpose is to help you access and remain in higher education.
