> If you're experiencing financial hardship we have some hardship funds that may be able to help.

## [Digital support fund](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/hardship/digital-support-fund-/)

Students experiencing financial hardship can get funding for IT equipment to help you engage with your studies.

## [Financial assistance fund](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/hardship/financial-assistance-fund/)

Students experiencing financial hardship can apply to the financial assistance fund for a one-off payment to help with living costs.
