> Living costs bursaries for UK first-year undergraduate students studying a single or joint honours theatre or English course.

Three bursaries of £3,000 are available for first-year UK undergraduates who are applying to study a single or joint honours theatre or English course.

[David Nicholls established the Nicholls Arts Bursary](/students/support/finances/scholarships/nicholls-arts-bursary/about/) to support gifted students of theatre as they pursue their studies.

## Eligibility

You can apply if you:

* are holding an offer or awaiting a decision to study a single or joint honours theatre course in the Department of Theatre or English in 2025/26
* have an assessed household income of £50,000 or less;
* have not previously been to university;
* are a UK first-year undergraduate.

## How to apply

The Nicholls Arts Bursary is part of the additional funding programme.

You can apply via the [additional funding page](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/scholarships/additional-funding/).
