> These scholarships are for Black UK applicants, towards your first year of undergraduate study and employability opportunities.

Up to 20 awards of £7,000 are available in 2025/26.

Successful candidates receive £2,000 for their first, second and third years. There is also a further £1,000 available as Career Employability funding.

Successful applicants will be enrolled on the Futures Scholarship programme. You will be supported by the Futures Scholarships Co-ordinator to access career guidance and advice on University opportunities for personal and professional development.

#### If you started before 2025/26

Your awards have a value of £3,500 if you started before 2025/26. You receive:

* £2,000 in the first year
* £750 in the second and third years

There is also a further £1,000 available as Career Employability Funding.

For information about the payment schedules from previous years, contact the Futures Scholarships Co-ordinator.

## Eligibility

You can apply if you:

* have one of the following categories of ethnicity:
  + Black African
  + Black Caribbean
  + Black Other
  + Mixed – White and Black Caribbean
  + Mixed – White and Black African
  + Other mixed background (to include Black African, Black Caribbean or Black Other)
* are a UK applicant for full-time undergraduate study
* have an offer (or are awaiting a decision) to study for an undergraduate degree starting in 2025/26
* have a household income of £50,000 or less as confirmed by the Student Loans Company

Students from any subject can apply. Read the full [eligibility criteria](/students/support/finances/scholarships/black-futures-scholarship/terms-and-conditions/).

## How to apply

Black Futures is part of the additional funding programme. Applications are made via the [additional funding page](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/scholarships/vertical-aerospace-black-engineering/).
