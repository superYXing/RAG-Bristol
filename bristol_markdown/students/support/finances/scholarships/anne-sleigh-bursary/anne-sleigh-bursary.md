> Bursaries to help female students from state schools to study physics and biology at the University of Bristol.

This award is for Home UK undergraduate female students who intend to start studying physics or biology.

Awards of Â£2,500 per year of study will be made to:

* two female students of physics for up to four years,
* and two female students of biology for up to four years.

## Eligibility

* Home UK undergraduate female students who intend to start studying physics or biology.
* Applicants should have an offer (or be awaiting a decision) to study at the University of Bristol.

Read the [full eligibility criteria](/students/support/finances/scholarships/anne-sleigh-bursary/terms/).

## How to apply

The Anne and Peggy Sleigh Bursary is part of the additional funding programme. Apply via the [additional funding page](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/scholarships/additional-funding/).
