> Funding for care leavers, estranged students and bereaved students.

Awards of £1,500 are available yearly and paid directly to students in two equal instalments.

## Eligibility

You must be a fully registered student to apply for this bursary.

You can apply if you are:

* a current Home/UK, full-time undergraduate, studying for your first degree.
* a[care leaver](/study/care-leavers/) or [estranged from your parents](/study/estranged-students/) or have no living parents.
* aged under 25 on 1 September of the year you start your course.

For the full eligibility criteria, read the [terms and conditions](/students/support/finances/scholarships/standalone-bursary/terms/).

## How to apply

You can apply by completing the Bristol Standalone Bursary [online application form.](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=MH_ksn3NTkql2rGM8aQVG3okPmcberBHt-iwi1k7fYJUMDdPSFk1UzJJSDFYSFhEOVJHQUE0UE5JVyQlQCN0PWcu&route=shorturl)

Applications for the 2025/26 academic year open on 15 September 2025.

You must be a fully registered student to be able to apply for this bursary

If you have received this bursary before, read the [terms and conditions](/students/support/finances/scholarships/standalone-bursary/terms/) for information on bursary renewal.

## Deadline

The deadline for new applications for the bursary in the 2025/26 academic year is 17 July 2026.

## Further details
