> Part of the Futures Scholarships programme, these awards are to help fund the first year of undergraduate study and employability opportunities for UK applicants.

This scholarship is not accepting new applications.

## If you started before 2025/26

This scholarship is not accepting new applications. If you started before 2025/26, your awards have a value of £3,500. You receive:

* £2,000 in the first year
* £750 in the second and third years

There is also a further £1,000 available as Career Employability Funding.

For information about the payment schedule for previous years, contact the Futures Scholarship co-ordinator.
