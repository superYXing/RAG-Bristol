> These scholarships are for UK applicants, towards their first year of undergraduate study and employability opportunities.

Applications for the 2025/26 academic year have now closed.

Up to 50 awards are available in 2025/26.

You will receive £2,000 in the first, second and third years. There is also a further £1,000 available as Career Employability funding.

#### If you started before 2025/26

You receive:

* £2,000 in the first year
* £750 in the second and third years

There is also a further £1,000 available as Career Employability Funding.

For information about the payment schedule for previous years, contact the Futures Scholarship Co-ordinator.

## Eligibility

You can apply if you:

* are a UK applicant for full-time undergraduate study
* have an offer (or are awaiting a decision) to study for an undergraduate degree starting in 2025/26
* have a household income of £50,000 or less as confirmed by the Student Loans Company.

Read the [full eligibility criteria](/students/support/finances/scholarships/futures-scholarship/terms/).

## How to apply

Applications are made via the [additional funding page](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/scholarships/additional-funding/). When you apply for additional funding, you complete one application form but are considered for a number of scholarships and bursaries.
