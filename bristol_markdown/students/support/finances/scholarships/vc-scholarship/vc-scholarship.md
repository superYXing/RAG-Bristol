> Supporting students with exceptional musical, dramatic or sporting talent within or outside of their field of academic study.

The Vice-Chancellor's Scholarship supports students with outstanding potential in drama, music, or sport.

The Vice Chancellor’s Scholarship provides an award of £1,552 in three instalments for one year.

Successful scholars may apply again in future years if they demonstrate exceptional ability and commitment while a recipient.

Awards are made on a competitive basis, with some minimum criteria. They are not means-tested.

## Eligibility

You can apply if you are:

* Intending to study at the University of Bristol in the 2026/27 academic
* an International or Home (UK) student.

You can apply for a Vice Chancellor’s Scholarship even if you are not studying or looking to study a course within its department.

Read the [full eligibility criteria.](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/scholarships/vc-scholarship/terms/)

## How to apply

Applications open on Wednesday 10 December 2025.

You can apply via the [online application form.](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=MH_ksn3NTkql2rGM8aQVGyaZAIvbt81KrKBR725vQtBUOE5OV0xVTFVWNUw4NjJWSDUzWFZJSUw1WS4u)

### References

You need to provide contact details for a reference as part of your application.

## Deadline

Applications for 2026/27 entry open at 8 am Wednesday 10 December 2025 and close at 11.59 pm Sunday 1 March 2026.

## Assessment process

Scholarship applications are considered by:

* the Music, Drama, or Sport department

Applicants who are successful will be invited to audition / interview for a place on the scheme. The Scholarships and Bursaries team aims to notify all applicants of the outcome of their application by Thursday 30 April 2026.
