> An award of £750 for new UK undergraduate students in their first year of study in the School of Dentistry.

Applications for the 2025/26 academic year have now closed.

Two new UK undergraduate students will be selected to receive the bursary, either on the BDS Dentistry course or the BDS Gateway to Dentistry course.

## Eligibility

You should:

* be a Home (UK) student;
* hold an offer to study either five-year BDS Dentistry (A206) course or six-year BDS Gateway to Dentistry (A208) course;
* be in your first year of study in 2025/26;
* have not previously studied to degree level;
* have a household income of £25,000 or under.

## How to apply

The Christopher Frederick Edwards bursary is part of the additional funding programme. To apply, go to the [additional funding programme page](/students/support/finances/scholarships/additional-funding/).
