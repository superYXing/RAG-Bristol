## Scam warning

You will never be asked to make a payment over the phone. [Visit your student financial statement](https://bristol.ac.uk/student-financial-statement?_gl=1*1jw6szc*_ga*MTE0MTA1ODk4Ni4xNjQ4NDU0NTMy*_ga_6R8SPL3HLT*MTY5MjM1MDA4OC43Ni4xLjE2OTIzNTM1MzcuNjAuMC4w) where you can pay by card or by bank transfer via a secure portal.

## Financial statement

[Pay your tuition and accommodation fees](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/pay-your-fees/) or view [your financial statement](https://bristol.ac.uk/student-financial-statement).

From your financial statement, you can:

* view your payment transactions
* view your sponsorship information
* view and amend your bank details
* where eligible, create a payment plan
* pay by card
* pay by bank transfer.

Payments can take a few days to appear on your financial statement.

[Watch a video tutorial](https://youtu.be/17E9vuJCsLY "Youtube video") on how to view and understand your financial statement.
