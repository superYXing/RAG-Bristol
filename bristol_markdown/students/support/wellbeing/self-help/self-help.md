> Find out how you can get advice and guidance when you have a problem at university so that you can move forward and get the most from your studies and your time in Bristol.

[## Book an appointment with a wellbeing adviser](/students/support/managing-student-life/book-an-appointment/)

Book an appointment with an adviser. They can offer information and advice, and work with you to help you get to where you want to be.

[## Advice about money](/students/support/finances/advice/)

Advice on managing your money as a student at Bristol. We offer online drop-in sessions with advisors on weekdays.

[## Request support for someone else](/students/support/managing-student-life/help-someone/)

Tell us if you are worried about someone else, so we can help them get the right support.

[## Get independent study advice](https://www.bristolsu.org.uk/support-centre/academic-advice)

Get independent advice about your studies from a dedicated team at Bristol SU.

[## General student enquiries](/students/support/help/)

You can contact our Student Services team with general questions about university and student life, or find out how to contact your school or faculty if you have a course or academic query.

[## Emergency help](/students/support/health/emergency-help/)

Emergency contact numbers and University out-of-hours contacts.

[## Student Counselling Service](/students/support/managing-student-life/counselling/)

The Student Counselling Service offers free, short-term counselling from qualified counsellors and therapists. Find out how to get a referral.
