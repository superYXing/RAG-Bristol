> If you, or someone you know, have experienced unwanted actions or practices that are inappropriate or unreasonable, find out how to report it and get support.

## Tell us about an unacceptable behaviour

**[Get advice on or report unacceptable behaviours](/students/support/health/report-unacceptable-behaviour/how-to-report/)**

If you have experienced unacceptable behaviour, we will provide you with support and information to help you decide what's next.

**[Emergency services](/students/support/health/emergency-help/)**

Know who to contact in an emergency and how to contact them.

## Get support for unacceptable behaviour

**[Request wellbeing support](/students/support/managing-student-life/book-an-appointment/)**

You may find things hard at university (for example, your studies or personal circumstances). You can speak to an adviser to work out a way forward.

**[Sexual violence support service](/students/support/health/sexual-violence-support-service/)**

We support students who have experienced any form of sexual misconduct, regardless of when or where it happened.
