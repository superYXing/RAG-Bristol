> Our dedicated teams are here to help you throughout your time at university.

## [Student finances](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/)

Help with managing your money and support for students in financial hardship.

## [Your wellbeing and student life](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/managing-student-life/)

Get help with managing challenges at university, book an appointment with a wellbeing adviser or find helpful advice on other aspects of student life.

## [Your physical and mental health](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/health/)

Find support for issues that are affecting your physical or mental health at university.

## [Disability support](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/)

Our advisers support students with a range of disabilities, learning differences and other health conditions.

## [Academic advice](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/academic-advice/)

What to do if you're thinking about suspending, transferring or withdrawing from your course.

## [Personal tutors](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/personal-tutors/)

Your personal tutor is an academic member of staff who you can meet regularly for help with your academic and personal development.

## [Peer mentoring](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/peer-mentoring/)

Peer mentoring is available for any first year undergraduate to help you settle in during your first few months.

## [Student visas](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/directory/visas/)

Advice for students on a study or short-term visas and their dependants.

## [Your student record](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/your-record/)

Update your personal details on your university record.

## [Sexual violence support service](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/health/sexual-violence-support-service/)

Find out how to get an appointment with a specialist case worker if you have experienced sexual violence.

## [Student IT services](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/it/)

Get familiar with the systems you will use and the digital tools available to support your studies.

## [Make a complaint](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/complaints/)

Guidance for registered students on how to make a complaint about any part of your university experience.

## [Help and contacts](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/help/)

Where to find contact information for our Student Services team and your school.

## [Faith and spirituality](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/multifaith-chaplaincy/)

Find a place of worship in the city and meet our chaplains.

## [Current students search](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/search/)

Search the current students' website for information and support.
