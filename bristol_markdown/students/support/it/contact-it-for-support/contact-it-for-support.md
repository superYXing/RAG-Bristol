> Where to go for help with IT.

## Support

* Our virtual [IT Service Desk](https://uob.sharepoint.com/sites/itservices/SitePages/contacts.aspx) is here to help with your IT queries and issues both on and off campus.
* You can also visit the [IT Counter](https://uob.sharepoint.com/sites/itservices/SitePages/it-counter.aspx) to seek technical help, or to collect and drop off IT devices and accessories.
* Help and support on a wide range of topics is available on our [IT Service website](https://uob.sharepoint.com/sites/itservices).

Support is University of Bristol (UoB) access only.
