> Let us know if you are concerned about someone else and we'll try to get them the right support.

## Life-threatening emergency

If someone's life is in danger, call the [emergency services on 999](tel:999).

If you are on campus, call 999 and then call Security Services on [+44(0)117 331 1223](tel:+441173311223).

## Request support for someone else

[Complete the Serious concerns form](https://enquiry.bristol.ac.uk/request-someone-support/) if you are concerned about a student.

This form is open for anyone to use.

If you cannot complete the form, [phone +44 (0)117 456 9860](tel:+441174569860) instead.

## After you complete the form

We will do our best to support the person you are concerned about. We may not be able to discuss specific details for confidentiality reasons.

Read [our confidentiality statement](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/health/policies/student-services-confidentiality-statement/) to find out how we handle and share information.

We may ask you to:

* speak to the person you are worried about
* agree that a wellbeing adviser can share your name with that person so they know why an adviser is calling.
