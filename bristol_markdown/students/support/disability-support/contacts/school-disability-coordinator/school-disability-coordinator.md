> Your School Disability Coordinator (SDC) is your contact in your academic school. If you are studying a joint honours course, you have two SDCs.

## Coordinating your support

Your SDC liaises with Disability Services and your school to implement your support, based on [your study support plan](/students/support/disability-support/study-support-plan/).

They can provide advice on:

* how the recommendations in your study support plan apply to your school's teaching and assessment practices
* physical access to teaching spaces, including labs and seminar rooms
* where teaching takes place or the distance between teaching venues
* Fitness to Practice questions for courses that lead to a professional qualification: dentistry, medicine, veterinary science, social work, or PGCE.
