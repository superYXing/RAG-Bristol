> You may be eligible for some public grants or bursaries that provide funding for students with a disability.

## Student Health Association

The [Student Health Association](http://www.studenthealthassociation.co.uk/bursaries/) gives bursaries (up to £500) to support disabled students to keep up with their studies. Students must be studying full-time (or nearly full-time).
