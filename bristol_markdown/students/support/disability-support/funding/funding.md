> Funding is available to help support you with your studies, whether you are a UK home or EU/international student.

## [Disabled Students' Allowance (DSAs) for UK home students](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/funding/dsas/)

All UK home students with disabilities should apply for DSAs to find out what support can be funded.

## [Funding for disabled EU/international students](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/funding/international/)

EU/international students with disabilities may receive University (DRF) funding for study support. Contact us about funding before you accept your offer or come to Bristol.

## [Public grants and bursaries](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/funding/public-grants/)

Check if you qualify for support from a charitable organisation, such as the Student Health Association.

## [Funding for assessments](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/funding/assessments/)

Funding information for ADHD and Specific Learning Difference assessments.

## [Funding for everyday living, personal or medical care](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/disability-support/funding/everyday/)

Check what funding options are available to you for your everyday living, personal or medical care.

## [Special support maintenance loan](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/student-loans/undergraduate/#special-support-loan)

You may qualify for more maintenance loan if you meet one of the disability criteria.
