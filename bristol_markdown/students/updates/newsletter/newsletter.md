> Our student newsletters aim to share relevant news and events with Bristol students.

We produce a variety of different newsletters, which are sent to your University email account.

If you do not think you are getting the right newsletters via email, [let us know](mailto:student-comms@bristol.ac.uk).

## **All** student newsletter

The All Student Newsletter is sent out every two weeks to all current students.

* First Years: [12 December edition](https://mailchi.mp/dd1d58358ce9/12-dec-ug1)
* Second and third years: [12 December edition](https://mailchi.mp/1f2d750f1d05/12-dec-ug2)
* Final years: [12 December edition](https://mailchi.mp/fcae396aa1b3/12-dec-finals)
* Postgraduates: [12 December edition](https://mailchi.mp/f549500187a5/12-dec-pgt)

We've chosen MailChimp in response to your feedback for a mobile-friendly newsletter; we hope you like it!
