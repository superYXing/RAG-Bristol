> Further explore your passion by progressing to a postgraduate degree at Bristol. Stay in the city you love and enhance your career prospects.

## [Come to an event](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/visits-open-days/)

Speak to academics and current postgraduate students. Learn more about your chosen programme and making the transition to postgraduate study.

## [Find a postgraduate programme](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/find-programme/)

Explore our postgraduate programme listings. Find the programme that matches your interests and career ambitions.

## [Learn about postgraduate funding](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/fees-and-funding/funding/)

Get information about funding your postgraduate degree. Learn how you could benefit from our 25% alumni discount.

Workshops and mentoring for under-represented students, transitioning from undergraduate to postgraduate taught study.

By staying at Bristol for postgraduate study you can:

* get a 25% alumni discount on your tuition fees
* build on your existing relationships with academics and collaborate on research
* stay within a cohort of students who are passionate about your chosen subject area
* keep enjoying living in Bristol and make your transition to postgraduate life easier
* maintain relationships with industry developed during your undergraduate study.

[Sign up for our emails](https://bristolpg.askadmissions.co.uk/emtinterestpage.aspx?ip=uobstudents) and we'll keep you up-to-date about:

* events for students who want to stay at Bristol,
* funding opportunities
* the benefits of continuing to be a part of this rich and diverse community.

### Guaranteed offers for final-year undergraduates

We offer final-year undergraduate students a [guaranteed conditional offer](/study/postgraduate/apply/guaranteed-offers/) to postgraduate taught programmes.
