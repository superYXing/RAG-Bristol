> Explore the extra-curricular activities on offer, from joining a society to volunteering.

## [Student societies](https://www.bristolsu.org.uk/student-groups)

Find an activity you enjoy, with more than 350 Students Union societies, sports clubs, groups, and support networks to choose from.

## [Mentoring and peer support](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/opportunities/mentoring-and-peer-support/)

Become a peer support mentor and help new students settle in during their first few months at university.

## [Become a PASS leader](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/opportunities/pass-leaders/)

PASS leaders oversee group study sessions for first-year students from their course.

## [Trans and non-binary mentoring](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/opportunities/trans-and-non-binary-mentoring/)

Our trans and non-binary mentoring programme offers students the opportunity to be supported by a mentor with similar lived experience.

## [Student leadership and representation](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/opportunities/student-leadership-and-representation/)

Have your say by becoming a course rep or standing for election to the JCR or as a Students Union officer.

## [Volunteering in the community](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/opportunities/volunteering-and-outreach/)

There are lots of opportunities for volunteering with local groups during your time at university, from schools work to environmental projects.

## [Sports leadership and volunteering](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/sport/participate/)

Our Time Out and Game Changers programmes give you the training and preparation you need to volunteer in sports leadership or coaching.
