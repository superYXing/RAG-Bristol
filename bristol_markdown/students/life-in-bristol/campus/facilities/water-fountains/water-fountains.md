> A guide to where you can access free drinking water on campus.

The University of Bristol supports [Refill Bristol](https://www.refill.org.uk/refill-bristol/) by providing access to mains drinking water points all over the campus. Staff, students and visitors can refill water bottles or cups and reduce the impact of single-use plastics.
