> Personal safety, using your UCard, replacement UCards and lost property.

## [Using your UCard](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/cardservices/currentstudents/)

Your UCard gives you access to University buildings and is needed to borrow books from the library.

## [Get a replacement UCard](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/cardservices/lostcards/)

Get in touch with Security Services if you believe your UCard has been lost or stolen.

## [Lost property](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/securityservices/lost-property/)

Who to contact if you think you have lost personal items around the University campus.

## [Personal safety](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/securityservices/personalsafety/)

We want you to have a safe and happy time while you are at university. This guide advises you on staying safe and looking out for one another.
