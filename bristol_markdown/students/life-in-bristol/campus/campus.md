> Find out more about student ID cards, room bookings and campus facilities.

## [What's on](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/events/)

Discover upcoming online and in-person events hosted by the University and our Students' Union (SU).

## [ID card and security](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/id-card-and-security/)

Your UCard will be issued to you on arrival at the University. You'll need it to access buildings and use the library.

## [Room bookings](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/directory/learning-facilities/room-booking/student-room-bookings/)

You can ask to book a room on campus for your student group, if the Students Union is unable to accommodate you.

## [Cafés](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/cafes/)

University Source café locations and opening times.

## [Campus facilities](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/facilities/)

Locate facilities around the university campus including showers, water fountains and bike parking.

## [Nursery](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/nursery/)

The day nursery is primarily for children of University staff and students. It is registered by Ofsted Early Years to take children aged from 0 to 5 years.

## [Student noticeboards](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/noticeboards/)

You can put up posters on the boards in the Arts and Social Sciences Library, Senate House Study Centre and Hawthorns refectory.

## [Catering for student led events](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/campus/student-catering/)

Please follow the correct process when booking catering for student led events across the University estate.
