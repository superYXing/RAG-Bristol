## Dining room packages

You can get two meals every day in the [residence dining room](/accommodation/about/catered-accommodation/) of your choice. 

There are two options: 

* 1 week (all residences): £75
* 38 weeks (one residence): £2,652.

## Source dining, cafe and bar packages

You can get £65 each week to spend in [residence dining rooms](/accommodation/about/catered-accommodation/) and all [Source cafés and bars](/students/life-in-bristol/campus/cafes/). Alcohol is not included. 

There are two options: 

* 1 week (all residences, cafes and bars): £55
* 38 weeks (one residence, all cafes and bars): £1,900.

## Buy a catering package

You can buy a 1 week package via the [UoB Source Catering App](https://onelink.to/6xw84b), from your chosen app store

You can buy a 38-week package from [the online shop](https://secure4.touchnet.com/C20992_ustores/web/store_main.jsp?STOREID=11). If it starts after September, the price is based on the number of remaining weeks (pro-rata). 

## Cancel a catering package

You cannot cancel a one-week catering package. 

To cancel a 38-week catering package, email [uob-catering@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:uob-catering@bristol.ac.uk). We will refund the remaining days on your package. 

## Individual meals

You can also buy individual meals at our [dining rooms](/accommodation/about/catered-accommodation/): 

* breakfast: £6.50
* lunch or dinner: £9.00.

You can pay at the till, using the UoB Source Catering App or debit card.
