> Find out how to cancel your University accommodation contract before your tenancy has started.

If you decide to not to live in University accommodation, you must cancel your room booking and follow the relevant guidance depending upon your individual circumstance.

If you do not follow this guidance, you may be liable for your full accommodation fee.

## Cancellation policy for:

* [Undergraduate students](#ugcancel)
* [Students allocated Chantry Court](#chantrycancel)
* [Students allocated Deans Court or Woodland Court](#dcwcancel)

## Undergraduate students

Before your tenancy start date, you can cancel your accommodation contract by emailing [student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk). You will be released from your tenancy agreement, and you will not be liable for any further payments.

After your tenancy start date, if you cancel your accommodation you still need to pay the full accommodation fee unless you find a replacement tenant. Find out how to [cancel your tenancy after the start date](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/cancel-your-tenancy/).

## Postgraduate students

You can cancel your accommodation contract before the tenancy start date by emailing [student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk).

If you cancel your booking within seven days of paying your £500 rent in advance, you will receive a full refund. You will not be liable for any further payments.

If you cancel your booking after seven days of paying your £500 rent in advance (but before the tenancy start date), you will not receive a refund for your rent in advance but you will not be liable for any further payments.

### Students who have been allocated Chantry Court

You can cancel your accommodation contract before the tenancy start date by emailing [cancellations@unitestudents.com](mailto:cancellations@unitestudents.com](mailto:cancellations@unitestudents.com) and [student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk).

If you cancel your accommodation contract within seven days of completing your room booking, you will receive a full refund for your £250 rent in advance payment (if applicable). You will not be liable for any further payments. 

If you cancel your accommodation contract after seven days of completing your room booking, you will remain liable for the full accommodation fee unless you find a replacement tenant. Read more on our [nomination](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/nomination-properties/) [properties](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/nomination-properties/) page.

### Students who have been allocated Deans Court or Woodland Court

You can cancel your accommodation contract before the tenancy start date by emailing [student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](mailto:student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk) and [student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-accommodation@bristol.ac.uk). You will not be liable for any rent payments.  

If you cancel your accommodation contract after your tenancy start date, you will remain liable for the full accommodation fee unless you find a replacement tenant. Read more on our [nomination](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/nomination-properties/) [properties](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/nomination-properties/) page.
