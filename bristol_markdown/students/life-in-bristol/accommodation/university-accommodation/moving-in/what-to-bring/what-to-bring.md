## What to bring

### Bedroom and bathroom

* bedding (duvet, duvet cover, pillows, pillowcases and sheets)
* towels
* toiletries
* bath mat (for en suite accommodation)
* laundry bag
* clothes hangers
* mains extension leads can be used, so long as the load on the extension is no more than 13 amps. Only one lead can be used per socket.

[Marlborough House](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/residences/marlborough-house/) residents do not need to bring bedding and towels, as they are provided.

### Kitchen (self-catered residences)

* plates and bowls
* mugs and glasses
* cutlery
* bottle/can opener
* saucepans
* frying pan.

For [Marlborough House](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/residences/marlborough-house/) residents, you do not need to bring any kitchen equipment, as this is provided.

### Kitchen (catered residences)

Catered blocks have basic pantry areas, so you should bring fewer items:

* plates and bowls
* mugs and glasses
* cutlery
* bottle/can opener.

### Other things to bring

* cleaning products, including washing-up liquid, sponges, cloths and tea towels
* stationery
* [TV licence](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/tv-licence/).

Bedding, Bathroom, and Kitchen essentials aren’t included. You can order everything you need now, and have it delivered before you arrive. Shop now [at UniKitOut](https://www.unikitout.com/?partner=University%20of%20Bristol) and save 10% off with code BRISTOL10.

Read our [residence guides](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/residence-guides/) to find out what is provided in the rooms and communal areas.

## Other items

### What you can bring

You are allowed to bring other items with you into halls, including:

* kettle (for kitchen/pantry use only)
* sandwich / toasty makers
* coffee machines
* George Foreman (or similar) grill (single not double unit only)
* rice cookers
* bread makers
* electric egg poachers
* electric whisks
* smoothy makers
* Juice extractors
* strings of lights / strip lights (battery operated only)
* disco balls
* music systems and speakers
* games console
* hair dryers and hair equipment
* TVs
* dehumidifiers
* fans
* clothes airers.

If you are planning to bring electrical items into your residence, read our [electrical safety guidance](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/health-safety-and-security/electrical-safety/).

### What you cannot bring

You are not allowed to bring:

* electric hot plates
* air fryers
* combination microwaves
* pressure cookers
* ice cream makers
* deep fat fryers
* fridges
* mini washing machine
* e scooters
* e bikes
* heated towel rails
* heated clothes airers
* humidifiers
* electric wax melters
* electric blankets
* sunbeds
* barbecues
* halogen / induction oven
* plug in air freshener
* lava lamps
* fan heaters
* pull up bars
* free standing lamps
* electric woks
* Portable/mini dishwasher.

### Cars

You should not bring a car to Bristol unless it is essential, for example, because of a disability. Find out more about [cars and parking permits](/accommodation/parking/student-parking/).
