## Bike storage

There is bike storage available at this residence.

Bike storage for Block A is located at the back of Block A.

Bike storage for Block B is located along the side of Whitehouse Lane.

Bicycles must not be brought into the house/flats due to fire regulations. Any bicycle found in the building, including in bedrooms, will be removed.

Find out more about [bike security and storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Cleaning, recycling and rubbish

You are responsible for the day to day cleaning of your accommodation. The [Student Cleaning Expectations and Guidance](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/media/docs/student-cleaning-expectations-and-guidance.pdf) document has some helpful information on what cleaning products to use and how, so you know what to do to ensure your accommodation is cleaned well and that you are maintaining a healthy environment.

Flat kitchens will be cleaned once every two weeks by staff.

Residents are responsible for waste removal and correct recycling. Information is provided in the flats and in the bin store. All bins are correctly signed.

Access to the bin store is located on the external part of the building.

Find out more about your [cleaning and recycling](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/cleaning-recycling-rubbish/) responsibilities in University accommodation.

## First aid

If you have an accident or near miss while in your accommodation, you can report it to your Student Support Centre. We can pass this information to the appropriate team so that steps can be taken to prevent it happening again.

Contact number: [+44 (0)117 428 3302](tel:+44117 428 3302)

Email: [student-services@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-services@bristol.ac.uk)

Visit: Open 7am to 7pm at Student Support Centre, Senate House, Tyndall Avenue, Bristol, BS8 1TH.

A near miss is an incident that could have resulted in an injury, such as slipping on the stairs or uneven flooring.

Your onsite accommodation team do not hold first aid boxes, so they are unable to provide you with bandages, dressings, plasters or any medication.

## Connect to the internet

### Wi-Fi connection

1. Connect to **ASK4 Wireless.**
2. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/).
3. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Wired connection

1. Connect the ethernet cable from the device to the wall socket.
2. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/).
3. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Add additional devices

1. On the new device, log into [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/).
2. Select **Enable this device.**

If you do not have a web browser on your device, you can add your device's MAC address at [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/) to get connected instead.

### Get the app

Install the ASK4 app to manage your account and get any help or support your need. Search 'ASK4 support' in the App Store or Google Play.

This content is also available at [support.ask4.com](https://support.ask4.com/).

## Laundry

The laundry facilities are located on the ground floor. Machines operated by [WashStation](https://washstation.co.uk/). You can add credit and pay via the WashStation app.

### Prices:

* Washing: £3
* Drying (25 minutes): £1.50

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Lost keys or locked out

If you are locked out, contact reception and a member of the residence staff will be able to let you back into your room. You will need to show them your photo ID.

Reception is manned 24/7: report to reception or call [+44 (0)117 454 4029](tel:+44117428330).

Charges may apply for lost keys.

## Lost property

Lost property will be held on site throughout tenancy. At the end of tenancy, it will be held for a further 30 days. Any unclaimed items will be given to charity.

## Parking

### Car parking

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

The postal address is for Block A is:

*Your name, your flat/room number, Metal Works, Dalby Avenue, Bedminster, Bristol, BS3 4FJ.*

The postal address is for Block B is:

*Your name, your flat/room number, Metal Works, Percy Street, Bedminster, BS3 4FL.*

Post will be delivered to reception and is available for collection from reception 24/7. You will need to show your  ID.

For post that needs a signature, please ensure that any delivery instructions state that reception staff can sign for the delivery on your behalf.

We may refuse delivery of a parcel that is too large or heavy, damaged, appears to have been opened or if it contains a prohibited item.

All post received for students who are no longer living at Metal Works will be destroyed.

## Request a repair

All repairs or maintenance requests must be reported to your residence. If you don’t tell us about an issue, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged.

You can request a repair or maintenance issue via [the Fresh student portal app](https://thisisfresh.securerc.co.uk/residentservices/metalworks/userlogin.aspx) or by informing a member of staff at Reception.

If you don’t tell us about an issue, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged.

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/) you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.
