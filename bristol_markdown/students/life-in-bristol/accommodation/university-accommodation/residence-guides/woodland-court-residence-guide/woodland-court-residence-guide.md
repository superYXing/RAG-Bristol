## Arrival information

Before you arrive, you need to [do the accommodation induction](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#induction "https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#induction") and [book an arrival time slot](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#arrival "https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#arrival").

Then you can [view your arrival instructions](https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/262F2406/27/1148/My_Accommodation_Ind-Accommodation_Induct "https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/262F2406/27/1148/My_Accommodation_Ind-Accommodation_Induct") in the Accommodation Portal.

Address: Woodland Court, 29 Belgrave Road, Redland, Bristol, BS8 2AA.

Telephone: [+44 (0)208 825 2179](tel:+442088252179).

Email [student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](mailto:student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk).

Opening hours for reception are 9 am to 5 pm.

Students moving in to halls in September 2025 can arrive from Saturday 13 September.

A key fob provides access to this residence.

## Bike storage

Bike storage is located at the back of Woodland Court behind Flats 13-16. There are also bike racks in the courtyard.

Bicycles must not be brought into the house/flats due to fire regulations. Any bicycle found in the building, including in bedrooms, will be removed.

Find out more about [bike security and storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Catering options

This residence is self-catered.

## Cleaning, recycling and rubbish

You are responsible for the day to day cleaning of your accommodation. The [Student Cleaning Expectations and Guidance](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/media/docs/student-cleaning-expectations-and-guidance.pdf) document has some helpful information on what cleaning products to use and how, so you know what to do to ensure your accommodation is cleaned well and that you are maintaining a healthy environment.

Communal kitchens will be cleaned once a week by staff.

Find out more about [cleaning, recycling and rubbish](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/cleaning-recycling-rubbish/) in University accommodation.

Find out more about [fire safety](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/health-safety-and-security/fire-safety/) in University accommodation.

The assembly point for Woodland Court is by Woodland Church, opposite the main entrance.

### Fire alarm testing

Fire alarms will be tested every Wednesday from 2 pm onwards. You do not need to evacuate during a fire alarm test.

## Connect to the internet

The internet provider for your residence is [ResNet](http://my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk).

To get wifi access using the ResNet network, visit [my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk](http://my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk) and follow the link to create a key.

A personal key will be emailed to you; do not share this with others. You are responsible for any use or misuse via this account.

## Laundry facilities and costs

Washing facilities are on the ground floor. Machines are operated by Wash and Go. You can pay using contactless card payments.

### Prices:

* Washing: £2.80
* Drying: £1.80

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Lost keys or locked out

If you are locked out, contact reception and a member of the residence staff will be able to let you back into your room. If on-site staff are unavailable, call the duty warden on [+44 (0)7990 643972](tel:+447990643972).

If you have lost your key you will be charged a fee for a replacement, unless your keys were stolen and you can provide a crime reference number from the police.

Reception telephone number: [+44(0) 20 8825 2179](tel:+442088252179)  
Email: [student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](mailto:student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk)

## Lost property

Lost property will be kept in reception until the end of the tenancy. Any unclaimed items will be given to charity.

## Parking

### Car parking permit

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

The postal address is: *Your name, your flat/room number, Woodland Court, 29 Belgrave Road, Bristol, BS8 2AA.*

All mail will be distributed to the letterbox for your flat. Larger parcels, or mail that requires a signature, will be held by reception. You will be contacted to let you know that there is an item you need to collect. Photo ID will be required.

Woodland Court will only accept parcels delivered by Royal Mail and Parcel Force. For parcels delivered by other companies, such as Amazon, Hermes or DPD, you must make sure you are available to accept the delivery when it arrives.

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/), you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us about something you have damaged, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.
