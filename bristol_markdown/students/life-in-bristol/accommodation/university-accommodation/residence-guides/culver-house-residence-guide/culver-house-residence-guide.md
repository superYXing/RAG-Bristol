## Bike storage

Bike storage is located near Reception on the ground floor and next to the entrance to the social spaces. You can access the bike storage in the lobby areas from either the front and back entrance.

Bike are stored on a first-come-first-served basis due to there being only 10 bike spaces available. Please ensure you bring your own bike lock.

Find out more about [bike storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Cleaning, recycling and rubbish

You are responsible for the day to day cleaning of your accommodation. ## Fire safety

Find out more about [fire safety](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/health-safety-and-security/fire-safety/) in University accommodation.

### Fire assembly point at Culver House

To the rear of the building on Culver Street (opposite Unite House).

### Fire alarm testing

Fire alarms will be tested every Tuesday at 2 pm. You do not need to evacuate during a fire alarm test.

If you hear a fire alarm at any other time or if the fire alarm sounds continuously for more than 10 seconds, you must evacuate the building immediately.

## Internet

### Wi-Fi connection

1. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/)
2. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Wired connection

1. Connect the ethernet cable from the device to the wall socket
2. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/)
3. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Add additional devices

1. On the new device, log into [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/)
2. Select **Enable this device**.

If you do not have a web browser on your device, you can add your device's MAC address at [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/) to get connected instead.

### Get the app

Install the ASK4 app to manage your account and get any help or support your need. Search **ASK4 support** in the App Store or Google Play.

This content is also available at [support.ask4.com](https://support.ask4.com/).

## Laundry facilities and costs

The laundry facilities are located in the building on the first floor to the left of the reception.

The machines are operated by an app that you can download when you arrive and you will then be able to top up and do your washing from the app.

### Prices:

* Washing: £3.70
* Drying: £2.20

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Lost keys or locked out

If you are locked out, press the intercom or phone our 24 hour emergency number for reception to access the building.

Reception telephone number: [+44 (0)117 954 4807](tel:+441179544807)  
Email: [culverhouse@capitolstudents.com](mailto:culverhouse@capitolstudents.com)

If you have lost your key, you will need to show ID to staff at reception.

If during office hours, you can be reissued a replacement key fob/card.

If outside of these times, you will be let into your flat and room once you have shown ID. You can go to reception to pick up a new key when reception is open. The office hours are usually 9 am to 5 pm Monday to Friday.

Outside of these office hours, Security provides 24 hour cover at the reception desk.

## Lost property

Lost property will be kept in reception until the end of the tenancy. Any unclaimed items will be given to charity.

## Parking

### Car parking

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

The postal address is: *Your name, your flat/room number, Capitol Students, Culver House, 26 Park Street, Bristol, BS1 5JA.*

All mail will be sorted at reception. Larger parcels, or mail that requires a signature, will be held by reception. You will be contacted to let you know that there is an item you need to collect.

All post received for students who are no longer living at Culver House will be destroyed.

## Rent and tenancy dates

The dates of your tenancy and total accommodation fee can be found in your tenancy agreement in the [Accommodation Portal](https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/).

Guidance for how and when to pay your fees can be found on the ['Pay your fees'](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/pay-your-fees/) page.

## Request a repair

All repairs or maintenance requests must be logged via the Capitol Students Maintenance Portal. The onsite maintenance team will respond to the issue as soon as possible.

If you don’t tell us about an issue, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged.

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/) you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.
