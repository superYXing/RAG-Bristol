## Arrival information

Before you arrive, you need to [do the accommodation induction](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#induction "https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#induction") and [book an arrival time slot](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#arrival "https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/moving-in/get-ready/#arrival").

Then you can [view your arrival instructions](https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/262F2406/27/1148/My_Accommodation_Ind-Accommodation_Induct "https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/262F2406/27/1148/My_Accommodation_Ind-Accommodation_Induct") in the Accommodation Portal.

Address: Deans Court, 3 St George's Road, Bristol BS1 5UL.

Telephone: [+44 (0)208 825 2179](tel:+442088252179) . Email [student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](mailto:student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk). 

Opening hours for reception are 9 am to 5 pm.

Students moving in to halls in September 2025 can arrive from Saturday 13 September.

## Access to the building

You can access this residence with a key fob.

## Bike storage

Bike storage is located in the basement below the south/west block next to the laundry room. Residents can access the bike storage via a code that can be obtained from reception.

Bicycles must not be brought into the house/flats due to fire regulations. Any bicycle found in the building, including in bedrooms, will be removed.

Find out more about [bike storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Catering options

This residence is self-catered, but [food and drink packages](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/kitchens-catering/#sourcecard) are available to purchase.

## Cleaning, recycling and rubbish

You are responsible for the day to day cleaning of your accommodation. The [Student Cleaning Expectations and Guidance](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/media/docs/student-cleaning-expectations-and-guidance.pdf) document has some helpful information on what cleaning products to use and how, so you know what to do to ensure your accommodation is cleaned well and that you are maintaining a healthy environment.

Communal kitchens are cleaned once a week by staff.

Find out more about your [cleaning and recycling](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/cleaning-recycling-rubbish/) responsibilities in University accommodation.

## Fire safety

Find out more about [fire safety](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/health-safety-and-security/fire-safety/) in University accommodation.

### Fire assembly point at Dean's Court

The assembly point for Dean's Court is located at the bottom of St. George's road by Brunel House.

### Fire alarm testing

Fire alarms will be tested every Friday from 2 pm. You do not need to evacuate during a fire alarm test.

## Insurance

Basic personal belongings are insured as part of your accommodation contract. Insurance for this residence is provided by Cover4insurance.

Check your policy and [arrange additional cover if required](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/contents-insurance/).

## Connect to the internet

The internet provider for your residence is [ResNet](http://my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk).

To get wifi access using the ResNet network, visit [my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk](http://my.resnet.bristol.ac.uk) and follow the link to create a key.

A personal key will be emailed to you; do not share this with others. You are responsible for any use or misuse via this account.

## Laundry facilities and costs

The laundry room is located on the lower ground floor.

The machines are operated by Wash and Go.

### Prices

* Washing: £2.80
* Drying: £1.80

You can pay for washing facilities with contactless card payments.

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Lost keys or locked out

If you are locked out or have lost your room key, contact reception and a member of staff will let you back into your room.

Reception telephone number: [+44 (0)20 8825 2179](tel:+442088252179)  
Email: [student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk](mailto:student.enquiries@a2dominion.co.uk)

If on-site staff are unavailable, call the duty warden on [+44 (0)7880 700264](tel:+447880700264).

If you have lost your key, you will be charged a fee for a replacement. You must pay the fee within seven days unless the original key is found.

## Lost property

Lost property will be kept at reception until the end of the tenancy. Any unclaimed items will be given to charity.

## Parking

### Car parking permit

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

### Your address and where to collect post

The postal address is: *Your name, your flat/room number, Dean's Court, St George's Road, Bristol, BS1 5UL*.

All post will be distributed to the letterbox for your flat.

For larger mail or parcels that need a signature, couriers will need to phone you so you can collect it from the entrance.

If you know you are not going to be in when a delivery arrives, you can arrange for the item to be delivered to one of several local collection points in the area. A member of staff can let you know where they are.

All post received for students who are no longer living at Dean's Court will be destroyed.

* call [+44 (0)208 825 2179](tel:+442088252179).

For out of office hours (9 am to 5 pm) or an emergency, phone [+44 (0)7880 700264](tel:+447880700264).

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/) you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.
