## Bike storage

Bike storage is located in the courtyard of Block A. This can be accessed using your key fob.

Bicycles must not be brought into the house/flats due to fire regulations. Any bicycle found in the building, including in bedrooms, will be removed.

Find out more about [bike storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Internet

### Wi-Fi connection

1. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/)
2. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Wired connection

1. Connect the ethernet cable from the device to the wall socket
2. Go to [signup.ask4.com](https://signup.ask4.com/)
3. Follow the signup process. You will need to provide your flat and room number.

### Add additional devices

1. On the new device, log into [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/)
2. Select **Enable this device**.

If you do not have a web browser on your device, you can add your device's MAC address at [myaccount.ask4.com](https://myaccount.ask4.com/) to get connected instead.

### Get the app

Install the ASK4 app to manage your account and get any help or support your need. Search **ASK4 support** in the App Store or Google Play.

## Laundry facilities and costs

* Print Hall 1: the laundry room is located on the lower ground floor.
* Print Hall 2: the laundry room is in the Common Room on the ground floor.

Machines are operated by Circuit. You can pay for washing facilities using either a top-up card or the Circuit App.

### Prices

Print Hall 1:

* washing: £3.70 to £4.70
* drying: £2.30

Print Hall 2

* Washing: £3.40 to £4.40
* Drying: £2

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Lost keys or locked out

If you are locked out, contact reception or call [+44 (0)117 375 1260](tel:+441173751260) and a member of the residence staff will be able to let you back into your room. You will need to show them your ID.

If you have lost your key, go to reception with your ID to get a replacement, for which you will be charged.

If you then find your old key and return the new one to reception within five days of it being issued, you will not be charged for the replacement.

Reception telephone number: [+44 (0)117 375 1260](tel:+441173751260)  
Email: [printhall@yugo.com](mailto:printhall@yugo.com).

## Lost property

All lost property will be stored in the lost property box in the parcel room. You can reclaim lost items from reception during opening hours. You will need to:

* provide an accurate description of the item
* describe when and where the item was lost
* show your ID
* sign for the item.

Items will only be stored for seven working days.

## Parking

### Car parking

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

The postal address is:

Print Hall 1:

*Your name, your flat/room number, Print Hall, 1 Temple Way, Bristol, BS2 0FX*.

Print Hall 2:

*Your name, your flat/room number, Print Hall, Unity Street, Bristol, BS1 0BX*

All mail will be distributed to the letterbox for your flat in reception.

Larger parcels, or mail that requires a signature, will be held by reception. You will be contacted to let you know that there is an item you need to collect.

You can collect parcels from reception between 8 am and 4 pm. Photo ID will be required.

If you do not collect your parcel within five working days, it will be returned to sender.

All post received for students who are no longer living at Print Hall will be returned to sender.

## Request a repair

All repairs or maintenance requests must be logged formally. If you don’t tell us about an issue, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged.

Report a maintenance issue or request a repair by:

* emailing [printhall@yugo.com](mailto:printhall@yugo.com)
* using the YugoApp.

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/), you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us about something you have damaged, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.

## Social spaces

Print Hall 1 has two common rooms, a quiet study space, and a gym located in Block A. These can be accessed using your key fob and is open 24/7.

Print Hall 2 has one study room and quiet study space.

In Print Hall 1 and 2, there are also courtyards which are open 24/7 and can be accessed with your key fob.
