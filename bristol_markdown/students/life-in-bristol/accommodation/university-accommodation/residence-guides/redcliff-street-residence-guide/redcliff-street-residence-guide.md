## Access to the building

A door code provides access to this residence.

## Bike storage

Bike storage is located outside in the car park.

Bicycles must not be brought into the house/flats due to fire regulations. Any bicycle found in the building, including in bedrooms, will be removed.

Find out more about [bike storage](https://bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/bike-storage/) in University accommodation.

## Catering options

This residence is self-catered.

## Cleaning, recycling and rubbish

Cleaning of flats (shared kitchen/living areas) is the responsibility of the residents. All bins, including recycling, are provided in each flat (in the shared kitchen/living areas).

The [Student Cleaning Expectations and Guidance](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/media/docs/student-cleaning-expectations-and-guidance.pdf) document has some helpful information on what cleaning products to use and how, so you know what to do to ensure your accommodation is cleaned well and that you are maintaining a healthy environment.

Find out more about your [cleaning and recycling](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/building-services-and-facilities/cleaning-recycling-rubbish/) responsibilities in University accommodation.

The internet provider for your residence is ASWeb.

Instructions on how to connect to the internet can be found in the welcome pack you are given on arrival.

## Laundry facilities and costs

Laundry facilites are within the shared kitchen. There is a washing machine which is free to use. There are no dryers.

You need to bring your own washing powder or tablets.

### Drying clothes

There are no dryers in this residence, so you may want to bring a drying rack to be used in a communal space.

You should not dry wet clothes in your room. This causes condensation and mould which can cause health problems, including some potentially serious lung infections.

## Parking

### Car parking

There is no parking available at this residence.

### E-scooters

The University [does not permit the use of private e-scooters](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/transportplan/transport/e-scooters/) and they must not be brought into residences or stored in bike storage areas.

## Post

The postal address is: *Your name, room/flat number, 92 Redcliff Street, Bristol, BS1 6LU.*

The postboxes are situated on the wall outside. There is no reception at this residence so you must be in to accept any deliveries.

## Rent and tenancy dates

The dates of your tenancy and total accommodation fee can be found in your tenancy agreement in the [Accommodation Portal](https://starrez.bristol.ac.uk/StarRezPortalX/).

## Request a repair

All repairs or maintenance requests must be reported to your residence. If you don’t tell us about an issue, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged.

You need to email [info@rengenlettings.co.uk](mailto:info@rengenlettings.co.uk) with a detailed description of the problem.

### Damage

If you [break or damage something in your residence](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/rent-contracts-regulations/damage/), you need to report it to us.

If you don't tell us about something you have damaged, it will be picked up at the end of your tenancy and you may be charged for the repair.
