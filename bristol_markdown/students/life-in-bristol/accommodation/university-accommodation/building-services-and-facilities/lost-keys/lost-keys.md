> Find out what to do if you lose your keys or get locked out of your University residence.

If you lose your keys or get locked out of your accommodation, help is available.

To find out who to contact if you are locked out or have lost your room key, please refer to your [Residence Guide](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/residence-guides/).
