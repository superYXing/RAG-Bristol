## Times of meals in dining rooms

Monday to Friday: 

* breakfast, 7.30 am to 9.30 am
* dinner, 5.30 pm to 7.30 pm.

Weekends:  

* breakfast, 8 am to 11.30 am
* lunch, 12 pm - 1.30 pm.

During Christmas and Easter vacations, you can use the dining rooms at Clifton Hill House or Churchill Hall.  

### Missed meals

If you need to miss dinner, you can get a packed meal.  

This includes a: 

* main dish
* salad
* cake or snack
* piece of fruit
* juice
* water.

You need to request packed meals from our catering team in advance.  

You can collect them: 

* at breakfast
* in the evening, after dinner.

## Allergies and food requirements

You need to tell us if you have any food allergies. Contact [uob-catering@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:uob-catering@bristol.ac.uk).

Find out about [our ingredients](/accommodation/about/catered-accommodation/) and menus.

## Guests and visitors

Anyone can eat in our dining rooms. You can buy [individual meals or catering packages](/students/life-in-bristol/campus/cafes/catering-packages/).

## Dress code

There is no formal dress code in our dining rooms, but your clothes should be suitable for a public space. You need to wear shoes.

## The UoB Source catering app

If you live in our [catered accommodation](/accommodation/about/catered-accommodation/) or have a [catering package](/students/life-in-bristol/campus/cafes/catering-packages/), you will get a digital meal card.

The card is in the UoB Source Catering App. To get the app:  

1. download the app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.itslollyltd.uobsourceapp&hl=en-US) or [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/uob-source-catering-app/id6554002517)
2. sign in with your university email address.

The app shows discounts on food and drink.
