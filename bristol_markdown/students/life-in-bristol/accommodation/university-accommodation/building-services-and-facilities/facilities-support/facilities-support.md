> What to do if you lose your room key, need minor first aid, or want to use your residence’s sports facilities.

If your residence is managed by the University, you can contact us for support with:

* accessing your room
* lost property
* signed post and deliveries
* using sports or leisure facilities.

Find out how to [get support with something else](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/).

## Contact us

Call [+44 (0)117 428 3300](tel:441174283300) at any time. Or email [residences-facilities@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:residences-facilities@bristol.ac.uk).

For in-person support, go to your Student Support Centre. Opening hours are Monday to Friday, 7am to 7pm.

## Other housing providers

If your residence is managed by a different housing provider, your Student Support Centre contact details are:

* phone: [+44 (0)117 428 3000](tel:+441174283000) (9 am to 4:30 pm)
* email: [student-services@bristol.ac.uk](mailto:student-services@bristol.ac.uk).

Opening hours are Monday to Friday, 9 am to 4 pm.
