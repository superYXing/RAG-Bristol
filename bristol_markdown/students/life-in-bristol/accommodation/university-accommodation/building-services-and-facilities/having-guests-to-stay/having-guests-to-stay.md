> The rules and procedures around having guests coming to visit you in accommodation.

Guests are welcome to stay with you in your accommodation providing the following measures have been met.

## **Register your guest**

You must register your guest at least 48 prior to their arrival. To register your guest, you need to:

1. Log in to the [Accommodation Portal](https://bristol.starrezhousing.com/StarRezPortalX/3B88D738/8/9/Login-Login).
2. Select on the *Guest Registration*.
3. You will be asked to provide the following information about your guest(s):
   * Name
   * Date of birth
   * Contact details
   * Arrival date and time
   * Departure date and time

## Rules and regulations for guests staying in accommodation

It is important that you and your guest(s) comply with the following conditions:

* Guests must be over the age of 16.
* Guests cannot stay any more than 3 consecutive nights except in exceptional circumstances.
* Your guest(s) must comply with the rules stated in your tenancy agreement.
* You are responsible for the behaviour of any guests for the duration of their stay.
* If your guest(s) cause any damage to University property, you are responsible for paying any damage costs.
* Should there be an issue that requires disciplinary action then one of the potential sanctions is to suspend your privilege to have visitors and/or guests.
* You will need to speak to your flatmates beforehand to ensure they are happy having other people stay in their home.
