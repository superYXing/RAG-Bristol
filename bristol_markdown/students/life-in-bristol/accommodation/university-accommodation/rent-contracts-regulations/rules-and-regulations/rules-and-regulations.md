## Accommodation management

University of Bristol-owned residences are managed in compliance with the [Universities UK Accommodation Code of Practice](https://www.universitiesuk.ac.uk/policy-and-analysis/Documents/2019/accommodation-code-of-practice.pdf). Partnership residences are managed in compliance with the [National Code for Assured Accommodation](https://www.nationalcode.org/).‌
