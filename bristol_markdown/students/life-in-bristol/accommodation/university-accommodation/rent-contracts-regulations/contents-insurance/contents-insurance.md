> Insurance for your personal items is part of your University accommodation contract.

If you live in a [University residence](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/university-accommodation/residence-guides/), you get contents insurance for some of your belongings. This is included in your accommodation contract.

You should [check your insurance policy](/directory/finance/insurance/information-for-students/insurance-for-students-in-university-accommodation/) and arrange extra cover if required.
