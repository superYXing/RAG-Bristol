> Find out how to apply for University accommodation for Semester 2. Tenancies run from 10 January 2026 to the end of the 2025/26 academic year.

[Study abroad programme](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/new/study-abroad/) students joining in January 2026 can apply for Semester 2 accommodation. This will be subject to availability.

The tenancy dates for Semester 2 accommodation will be from 10 January 2026 to the 2025/26 tenancy end date for the residence. To view the tenancy end dates, visit [our residences pages](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/residences/).

View our [Semester 2 accommodation costs for January entry 2025/26](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/about/costs/semester-two/).

## When to apply

Applications for semester 2 accommodation open on 16 October 2025.

Applications close on 14 November 2025.

## How to apply

To apply for Semester 2 accommodation, you must have received and accepted a firm offer of study from the University.

Follow our instructions on [how to apply for accommodation](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/apply/how-to-apply/).

## Finding private rented accommodation

For students who are not guaranteed an offer of University accommodation, we recommend that you look for accommodation in the private sector as a priority.

For detailed resources and house-hunting tips, visit the [finding private rented accommodation](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/accommodation/privately-rented/finding-privately-rented-accommodation/) pages.
