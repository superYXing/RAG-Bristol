> If you want to apply to stay in a University residence, how and when you can apply will depend on what type of student you are, and when you are looking to move in.

[## Returner accommodation](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/apply-to-stay-in-university-accommodation/returner-accommodation/)

If you have previously lived in University accommodation or studied at the University, you can apply for returner accommodation.

[## Accommodation for Semester 2 only](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/apply-to-stay-in-university-accommodation/semester-2-accommodation-/)

Find out how to apply for University accommodation for Semester 2 only (from 24 January 2024 to the end of the 2023/24 academic year).

[## Emergency accommodation](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/apply-to-stay-in-university-accommodation/emergency-accommodation/)

Find somewhere to live in an emergency, for example if your current accommodation is affected by a fire or flood.

[## Short-term summer accommodation](/students/life-in-bristol/accommodation/apply-to-stay-in-university-accommodation/short-term-summer-accommodation/)

Apply to stay in a University residence during the summer vacation.
