> How to get to exam venues off campus.

Most exams are held in venues on the University campus. In main exam periods we may also use some external venues including Coombe Dingle Sports Centre.

## How to get to Coombe Dingle Sports Centre

Find [Coombe Dingle](http://www.bris.ac.uk/maps/google/?latlng=51.489508,-2.6317810000000463&t=0&h=1) on our University Google map. Venue postcode: **BS9 2BJ**

Use the [Travel west website](http://travelwest.info/)or the [Traveline South West Journey Planner](http://www.travelinesw.com/) to plan your journey by public transport.

### Walking

Coombe Dingle is around a 10-minute walk from Hiatt Baker halls.

### By bicycle

There are cycle hoops at Coombe Dingle Sports Centre.

### By bus

The [Bristol Unibus U1](https://www.firstbus.co.uk/bristol-bath-and-west/routes-and-maps/bristol-universities/university-bristol) service travels directly from campus to the Stoke Bishop Transport Hub, which is around a 10-minute walk from Coombe Dingle Sports Centre.

You may find the [other First Bus timetable](https://www.firstbus.co.uk/bristol-bath-and-west/plan-journey/timetables/?operator=3&page=1&redirect=no) pages useful.

### By train

Coombe Dingle is around a 25-minute walk from Sea Mills railway station on the Severn Beach Line. Use the [Great Western Railway website](https://www.gwr.com/travel-information/train-times) to plan your journey.

### By car

There is free parking at Coombe Dingle Sports Centre.
