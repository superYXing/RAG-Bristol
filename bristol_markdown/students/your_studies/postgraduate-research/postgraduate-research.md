> Information for postgraduate research (PGR) students. Support with progression, PGR student life, and funding.

## [Research skills and development](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/doctoral-college/ppd/)

At the Bristol Doctoral College we have a wide programme for PGR students to support you in your work. This includes workshops, seminars, and online resources. The PGR Essentials Guide helps you to select the right training and development for each stage of your degree.

## [Postgraduate research supervision](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/doctoral-college/guidance/postgraduate-research-supervision/)

We have a range of tools and advice on the Bristol Doctoral College website. This can support you and your supervisor through the research the supervision process.

## [Annual progress monitoring (APM)](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/postgraduate-research/apm/)

The annual progress monitoring review process gives you the chance to reflect on your progress and confirm your plans for your next year of study.

## [Assessments and exams](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/postgraduate-research/assessments/)

Guidance on examiners, submitting the dissertation (thesis), viva (oral exam), receiving corrections (feedback), resubmission payment, uploading to the library and delaying public access.

## [Leave and absence](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/postgraduate-research/annual-leave/)

Information about taking leave or absence from your postgraduate research. This includes types of leave and short-term absence.

## [Extend your studies](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/academic-advice/extend-your-studies/)

What to do if you are a PGR student and thinking about extending your studies.

## [Postgraduate research stipends, tuition fees, and support grants](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/finances/pg-stipend-process/)

Discover how to set up and receive your postgraduate research stipend, and find out what to do if there are changes to your programme.

## [Connect with your PGR community](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/doctoral-college/events-and-community/)

Browse the PGR events calendar and join a PGR club or network. You can also find out about the PGR Hub, a dedicated events, meeting and social space in Senate House, just for PGR students.

## [Raising an issue related to your postgraduate research](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/doctoral-college/guidance/support-for-postgraduate-research-students/who-to-contact-about-issues-related-to-postgraduate-research/)

Information about what to do if you are having an issue related to your research. This includes advice about who to contact and current contact details.
