> Address, opening hours, facilities and services that are available at the Brambles Study Centre.

## Winter break closure

Closed 20 December to 4 January inclusive

Study spaces in the Brambles Study Centre cannot be booked.

Access is via Ucard only.

## Brambles Study Centre's opening hours

Monday to Friday: 9 am to 10 pm

## Brambles Study Centre's address

14 to 16 Woodland Road, Bristol, BS8 1UQ

## Accessibility

Contact [Library support](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/accessibility-support/) if you want to discuss:

* access
* any extra support you may need
* personal emergency and evacuation plan
