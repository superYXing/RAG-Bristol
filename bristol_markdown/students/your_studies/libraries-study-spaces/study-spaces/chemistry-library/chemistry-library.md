## Supported self-service

The Chemistry Library is a supported self-service Library.  
Library staff do checks of the space Monday - Friday 10-10:30am and 3-3:30pm. Outside of these hours telephone, Live Chat, and email help is available.

## Loan periods and borrowing limits

**All Chemistry Library items are subject to the [standard loan periods and borrowing limits](/library/use/borrowing/) with the following exceptions**

| Material | Loan periods and restrictions |
| --- | --- |
| New books on display, all journals, reference books, examination papers, theses | Confined to the library |
| Newcomers Collection books | All users - three-day loan |

[## Subject Librarians](/library/subject-support/subject-guides/)

Get expert advice and support from your subject librarian at any point during your studies! Your subject librarian is the go-to person for help with:

* accessing library resources
* finding and evaluating information
* referencing
* recommending books or ebooks to add to our collection
