> Address, opening hours, facilities and services that are available at the Richmond building.

## Richmond Building's opening hours

Monday to Friday:   
8 am to 11 pm

Saturday:  
9 am to 10 pm

Sunday:  
2 pm to 10 pm

## Facilities and services

The following facilities and services are available at the Richmond building:

* staffed
* silent study
* group study
* online discussion is allowed
* printing
* scanning
* cafe
* vending machine

## Richmond Building's address

105 Queens Road, Bristol, BS8 1LN

## Accessibility

Contact the [Library support](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/accessibility-support/) if you want to discuss:

* access ([or see AccessAble](https://www.accessable.co.uk/university-of-bristol/access-guides/richmond-building-city-of-bristol))
* any extra support you may need
* personal emergency and evacuation plan
