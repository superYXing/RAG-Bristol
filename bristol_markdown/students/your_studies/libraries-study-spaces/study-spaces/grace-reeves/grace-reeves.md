> Address, opening hours, facilities and services that are available at the Grace Reeves Study Centre.

## Closure notice

This study centre will be closed from Monday 2 to Wednesday 4 February for important maintenance work.

## Grace Reeve Study Centre's opening hours

Monday to Friday:  
8 am to 10 pm

Saturday and Sunday:  
closed

## Facilities and services

The following facilities and services are available at the Grace Reeve's Study Centre:

* silent study
* group study
* staffed
* digital study pods
* printing and scanning
* vending machine

## Grace Reeves Study Centre's address

University Road, Bristol, BS8 1SS

## Accessibility

Contact the [Library support](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/accessibility-support/) if you want to discuss:

* access ([or see AccessAble](https://www.accessable.co.uk/university-of-bristol/access-guides/geographical-sciences-city-of-bristol))
* any extra support you may need
* personal emergency and evacuation plan
