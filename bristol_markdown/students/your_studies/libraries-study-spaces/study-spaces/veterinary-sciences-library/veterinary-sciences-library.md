## Library closed 5pm 23 Dec - 9am 12 Jan

The Veterinary Sciences Library will be closed 5pm 23 Dec - 9am 12 Jan due to electrical work in the Churchill building. We apologise for the inconvenience. Other Langford study spaces will be open as usual.

## Opening hours

The Veterinary Sciences Library is open 24/7 for members of the Vet School with Ucard access to the Churchill Building.  

* Library staff are available for queries Monday to Friday 9am-5pm, (please note the office closes for lunch from 1-2pm).

## Loan periods and borrowing limits

**All Veterinary Sciences Library items are subject to the [standard loan periods and borrowing limits](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/use/borrowing/ "Borrowing") with the following exceptions**

| Material | Loan periods and restrictions |
| --- | --- |
| Serials | All users - seven-day loan |
| New books on display, reference books, books with red tape around the spine, theses, current issues of journals | Confined to library |
