## Medical Library Evening and Weekend Access

The Medical Library is accessible via an alternative route from **6pm on weekdays and all day at weekends**. To find this entrance, please follow the purple signs located at the top and bottom of the Cantock Steps, to the right of the Biomedical Sciences Building.

### Reduced services

During periods with reduced services only reading, reference, loan and return facilities are available. When the library is closed a book return box is available.

| Day | Reduced services |
| --- | --- |
| Monday to Friday | After 5 pm |
| Weekend | All Day |
