> Address, opening hours, facilities and services that are available at the Senate House study centre.

## Winter break closures

Closing at 8 pm on Monday 22 and Tuesday 23 December

Closed 24 December to 26 December inclusive

Open 10 am to 6 pm Saturday 27 to Monday 29 December

Closed 30 December to 4 January inclusive

Study spaces at the Senate House Study Centre cannot be booked.

Access is via Ucard only.

## Senate House's opening hours

Monday to Friday:   
8 am to 10 pm

Saturday and Sunday:  
9am to 6pm

## Facilities and services

The following facilities and services are available at the Senate House study centre:

* group study
* online discussion is allowed
* printing
* scanning
* cafe
* vending machine

## Senate House's address

Tyndall Avenue, Bristol, BS8 1TH

## Accessibility

Contact [Library support](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/accessibility-support/) if you want to discuss:

* access ([or see AccessAble](https://www.accessable.co.uk/university-of-bristol/access-guides/senate-house-city-of-bristol))
* any extra support you may need
* personal emergency and evacuation plan
