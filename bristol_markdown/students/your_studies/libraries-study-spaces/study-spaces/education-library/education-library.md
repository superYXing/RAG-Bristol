## Supported self-service

The Education Library is a supported self-service Library.  
Library staff do checks of the space Monday - Friday 10-11am and 2-3pm. Outside of these hours telephone, Live Chat, and email help is available.

## Loan periods and borrowing limits

**All Education Library items are subject to the [standard loan periods and borrowing limits](/library/use/borrowing/) with the following exceptions**

| Material | Loan periods and restrictions |
| --- | --- |
| Books in heavy demand | All users - seven-day and one-day loan |
| Serials | All users - seven-day loan. Some serials are one-day loan or confined to library |
| New books, reference books, theses, current issues of journals, counselling journals | Confined to library |
|  |  |

[More about borrowing](/library/use/borrowing/)

[## Subject Librarians](/library/subject-support/subject-guides/)

Get expert advice and support from your subject librarian at any point during your studies! Your subject librarian is the go-to person for help with:

* accessing library resources
* finding and evaluating information
* referencing
* recommending books or ebooks to add to our collection
