## Develop your study skills

We offer a range of options to help you develop your study skills; such as:

* [visit the study skills drop in hub](https://www.ole.bris.ac.uk/bbcswebdav/courses/Study_Skills/whats-on/index.html#/id/632acb7dfb7af103d447e4c6)
* [book a tutorial](https://www.ole.bris.ac.uk/bbcswebdav/courses/Study_Skills/whats-on/index.html#/id/5f044158cf57ca06c433abe4) (one to one session)
* [ask a question](https://uob.padlet.org/studyskills2/study-skills-ask-a-question-trhl5rl5cvggylnj)
* [attend workshops and study groups](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/study-support/study-skills/workshops/) (takes place in small groups)

For more help, see [our study blog](https://studyskills.blogs.bristol.ac.uk/) and [study resources](https://rl.talis.com/3/bristol/lists/CB4559BE-0D63-7B38-B410-4EC942B2DB46.html) (list of books).
