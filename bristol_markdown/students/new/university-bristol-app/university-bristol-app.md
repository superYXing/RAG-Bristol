## Get help with the University app

### Recording attendance

Troubleshoot using our guide to [common issues with recording attendance](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/teaching-and-learning/registering-attendance/#problems).

If you have not been able to [record your attendance](/students/your-studies/teaching-and-learning/registering-attendance/), you must contact your school.

### Other technical problems

Visit the [IT status page](https://itstatus.bristol.ac.uk/) to see if the team is aware of the problem.

If the problem is not recorded on the IT status page, you can [report a fault](https://uob.haloitsm.com/portal/).

## What the student app can do

Download the University of Bristol app to:

* [Record your attendance](/students/your-studies/teaching-and-learning/registering-attendance/): check in to lectures, seminars, labs, or any other classes. You can also set reminders to record your attendance.
* [View your timetable](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jz_7kk5bCrg&list=PLFZ6Q-G4wIXD29OBwoIti21zc1F267wD3&index=2) (Youtube video): including important academic dates and University events.
* [Get directions around campus](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b82zE-iC8l0&list=PLFZ6Q-G4wIXD29OBwoIti21zc1F267wD3&index=5) (Youtube video): use Mazemap to find teaching rooms and points of interest like printers and cycle parking.
* Access Blackboard with all of your learning resources, assignments, and grades.
* Find and book your nearest computer, study seat, or teaching space.
* Buy print credits.

This is not a complete list.

### Optional tiles

Optional tiles include:

* University bus tracker.
* UOB Active app.

This is not a complete list.
