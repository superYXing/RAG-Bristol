> Activities and resources to get you ready to start your studies.

## [Upgrade to University study skills module](https://www.ole.bris.ac.uk/bbcswebdav/courses/Study_Skills/upgrade-to-university/index.html)

Adjusting to learning at university may take some practice. Find out what skills you need to get the most out of your studies at university.

## [Digitally ready](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/digital-education/students/)

A digital induction course for students to refresh their basic digital skills.

## [Library and study spaces](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/library/)

Find out what library services we offer and get familiar with our study spaces, where they are and when they are open.

## [Study support](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/your-studies/study-support/)

We have a wide range of free study support available including study skills, peer-assisted learning, and language courses.

## [Get IT ready](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/support/it/)

Get familiar with the IT systems you will use and the tools available to help you get the most out of your learning.

## [Study skills](https://www.ole.bris.ac.uk/webapps/portal/execute/tabs/tabAction?tabId=_80766_1&tab_tab_group_id=_315_1)

Try our free online study skills resources to help you improve your academic skills. They are self-study so you can study at your own pace at a time that suits you.

## [Jumpstart University](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/education-development/jumpstart-university/?filter=date/list/all/30447,30453/none/none/none/all)

Free resources from The Open University curated with the Russell Group to help you take your first steps to university. Articles, activities and free courses.

## [Open units](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/students/new/get-ready-to-study/open-units/)

Learn about open units, what is available, and whether you can take an open unit as part of your course.
